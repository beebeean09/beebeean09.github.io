Chicken Fried Rice

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions

Servings: 6


Description

A savory, sweet version of chicken fried rice! With the Teriyaki marinated chicken added to a traditional fried rice recipe, this recipe is quick, easy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 (18 oz) bottle Teriyaki sauce
  • 3 Tablespoons sesame oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots (thawed)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2 eggs, slightly beaten
  • 3 cups cooked white rice (day old or leftover)
  • 3-4 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • Drizzle of sesame oil (Optional)

      • Directions

      1. Cook chicken covered in teriyaki sauce in a crock-pot on high heat for 3 hours. OR in oven at 375 FÂ° for 35 minutes.
      2. Once cooked, slice into small pieces. Set aside.
      3. Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the peas/carrots mix, onion and garlic. Stir fry until tender.
      4. Lower the heat to medium-low and push the mixture off to one side. Pour eggs on the other side of skillet. Stir fry until scrambled.
      5. Now add the rice and soy sauce and blend all together well. Stir in cooked chicken and cook until mixture is heated through.
      6. Drizzle sesame oil at the end if desired. Serve and enjoy!
